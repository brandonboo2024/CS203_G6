# Chatbot Usage Guide

## Quick Start

### Accessing the Chatbot
1. Log in to your TariffKey account
2. Look for the **purple floating button** in the bottom-right corner
3. Click it to open the chatbot window

### Closing the Chatbot
- Click the button again to close the chat window
- Or navigate to a page using the action buttons (auto-closes)

## What Can the Chatbot Do?

**What to say:**
- "calculate tariff"
- "how much does it cost"
- "tariff calculator"

## Features

### Message History
- Your conversation is saved during your session
- Scroll up to see previous messages
- Use the **trash icon** in the header to clear the chat

### Timestamps
- Each message shows when it was sent
- Helps you track your conversation

### Auto-Close
- When you click a navigation button, the chat automatically closes
- The page you requested will load

### Responsive Design
- Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- On mobile, opens full-screen for better experience

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see the chatbot"
- Make sure you're logged in
- Look for a purple round button in the bottom-right corner
- Try refreshing the page

### "The chatbot doesn't understand me"
- Try using simpler words
- Use keywords like: calculate, simulate, history, profile
- Type "help" to see available features

### "Action buttons aren't working"
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### "Chat window is too small/large"
- Desktop: Fixed size optimized for readability
- Mobile: Automatically adjusts to screen size
- Try rotating your device (mobile)

## Privacy & Data

### What's Stored?
- Messages are stored **only during your current session**
- When you close the browser or log out, chat history is cleared
- No conversation data is sent to servers

### What's Shared?
- **Nothing!** The chatbot runs entirely in your browser
- No chat data is transmitted or logged
- Your conversations are completely private

## Getting More Help
